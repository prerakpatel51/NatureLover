I've always loved plants. I remember when I was a kid, I would spend hours in my backyard, tending to my little garden. I loved the feeling of taking care of something living, and I was always fascinated by the different types of plants and how they grew.


As I got older, I started to learn more about plants and plant care. I read books, took online courses, and even started a blog about my plant-care journey. I quickly realized that I had a knack for helping people with their plants. I loved giving advice and helping people find the perfect plant for their home.


A few years ago, I decided to start a plant selling website. I wanted to share my love of plants with others, and I wanted to help people find the perfect plants for their homes.
